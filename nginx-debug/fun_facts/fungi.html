Did you know?

Fungi are all about funghal filaments, that's where all the magic is happening.
When you imagine a mushroom, you probably picture one with a cute capped one.
Well, the whole mushroom body is just for reproduction.
Everything it needs to do for survival is actually happening underground
in the funghal filaments!

Before trees were a thing, fungi were already conquering the dry land.
These "fungi-trees", called prototaxites, grew to over 20 feet tall!
